Miscellaneous

  1. National Sport: Ball Hockey
  2. National Bird: Chakor
  3. National Animal: Markhor
  4. National Flower: Jasmine
  5. National Anthem: The national anthem is known as the “Quami Tarana” in Pakistan, and is also called “Pak Sarzamin”, meaning “The Sacred Land”. Its music was composed by Ahmad G. Chagla in 1949 and lyrics were written by Hafiz Jullundhri in 1952, and was officially adopted as Pakistan’s National Anthem in August 1954. Click here to listen to the Anthem.
