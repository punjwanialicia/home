All About Pakistan

Pakistan, officially named the Islamic republic of Pakistan, is bordered by India, Afghanistan, Iran and China and is located in the continent of Asia, in the Middle East, near the Arabian Sea and the Gulf of Oman.It has a population of about 193.1 million, however it’s capital, Islamabad had a population of about 1.152 million in 2011. The main cities of Pakistan are Karachi and Lahore, and get a lot of tourists every year due to the amazing tourist sites and places they have.
The currency that is used there is Rupees, and in today’s time 1CAD is equal to 83.32PKR (Pakistani Rupees). Just like how 100 cents make up a dollar in Canada, in Pakistan, 100 paisa make up 1 Rupee. There used to be coins available of 1,2,5,10,25 and 50 Paisa and a note of 5 rupees but now aren’t in the market. However, there are coins od 1,2 and 5 rupees, and notes of 10,20,50,100,500,1000 and 5000 rupees.
Pakistan’s climate stays dry and hot throughout the year near the coast, but becomes cooler towards the northeastern uplands. The winter season is usually a bit chilly and dry, and begins November, throughout the end of January, From February till the start of March is spring, when it starts to become a little warmer and from March all the way to end June, summer has taken over, where temperatures might even reach 49℃. However, the monsoon period between June and September still is pretty humid but you can also feel the cool winds sometimes. Rainfall can very radically from year to year, and floods and drought aren’t an uncomming thing to hear about.
The official language of Pakistan is urdu. There are many other provincial languages spoken as well for example Sindhi, Punjabi, Balochi, Farsi, etc.
There are 4 major provinces and 2 Federaly Administered Areas. They are as follows:
Sindh, Punjab, Balochistan and Khyber Pukhtunkhwa, and the Federaly Administered Tribal Areas and the Area of Jammu and Kashmir.
The national flag of Pakistan is a green field with a white crescent moon and five-rayed star at its center, and a vertical white stripe at the hoist side. The flag was designed by Amiruddin Kidwai, and is based on the flag of All-India Muslim League. The flag is referred to in the national anthem as the Flag of the Crescent and Star, or as known as “Sitarah o-Hilal”. The banner above is a picture of the flag.
Pakistan’s traditional and authentic food are:
Biryani, Haleem, Chicken/Mutton Karhai, Halwa Puri, Kababs/Naan, Chicken Tikka, and Lassi. Tea is also drank in a wide range througout the country.
